About Westlake Encore

Westlake Encore Condos is the final phase of Etobicoke's first true urban village and fastest growing community; Westlake Village. Designed by Page + Steele / IBI Group Architects, Westlake Encore stands 45 storeys tall, is adjacent to Mimico Creek and anchors the north end of the site. rom its position, the tower overlooks the beautiful landscape of umber Bay Park and the shorelines of Lake Ontario.

Westlake Encore Condos is grounded by a 7 storey podium and is comprised of stylish condominium and townhome residences. Inspired by a desire to live life in balance, the residences at Westlake Encore Condos are conveniently connected to the many shops and services of the Village as well as Club Encore; a health and wellness facility which features indoor and outdoor amenities including a professionally equipped gym, and outdoor pool with rooftop cabanas. The Village at Westlake is the first design-built urban village in Etobicoke's burgeoning waterfront neighbourhood.
